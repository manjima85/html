CAR WIKIPEDIA

1. Hatchback

A hatchback is a car type with a rear door that opens upwards. They typically feature a four-door configuration, excluding the rear door.

2. Sedan

Out of the different types of cars, a sedan (US) or a saloon (UK) is traditionally defined as a car with four doors and a typical boot/ trunk.
